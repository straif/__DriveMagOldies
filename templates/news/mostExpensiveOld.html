So, after analysing this table some conclusions can be drawn: Oldest, cheapest, most expensive, newest, brands ...

  • The oldest car ever sold was a De Dion, Bouton et Trepardoux Dos-a-Dos Steam Runabout "La Marquise", built in 1884
  • The cheapest car were a 1939 Alfa Romeo Tipo 256 Cabriolet Sportivo and a 1931 Bugatti Type 51
  • The most expensive wheels ever auctioned was a Alfa Romeo 8C 2900B Lungo Spider, built in 1939
  • The most common brand auctioned was clearly Mercedes-Benz 540K Special Roadster
